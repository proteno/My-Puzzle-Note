The 18 ProTangram Tiling Game

  1. The 18 ProTangram is an interesting and excellent puzzle game for the purpose of education at school and of recreation at home. Players of different ages can practice and enhance their concept of reflective or rotational symmetry, and of the congruent subsets. The different varieties of the 18 ProTangram puzzle game provide an ideal opportunity to train and build up the ability in Combination, Optimization, Dissection, Tiling and Similarity.

  2. The 18 ProTangram Tiling Game contains two sets of patterns, four pieces of Tritans (Triaboloes) and fourteen pieces of Tetratans (Tetraboloes). These 18 pieces are composed of three or four isosceles right triangles and joined either at their sides or hypotenuses in all possible way. With the delicate pattern structure and the square grid, these 18 pieces are named as letters A, B, C, D, F, G, H, I, J, K, M, Q, R, S, V, W, X, and Z respectively. Using all or some of these 18 pieces, the players are challenged to fit the pieces into various fixed frame.

  3. There are five levels of complexity and difficulty from: BASIC, EASY, HARD, DIFFICULT, and EXPERT level. Twelve board games in a CD case have been designed.

  4. Contain small parts. Not intended for children under 6.
