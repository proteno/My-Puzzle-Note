• Q: How to use 12 Pentominoes to cover a cube ?
  • Rules: The cubenet must be assembled by 6 congruent shapes , and the 14 vertices of all cubenet superpose with at least one unit square's corner.
  • Hints: Try 4460 pcs of 10-ominoes ( no hole ) to connect a cube net and then tiling with 12 pentominoes.
